Privacy Policy for FREE LINK SHORTENER

1. Information We Collect

Our app collects the following information:

  • URLs that you input for shortening
  • Usage statistics (number of URLs shortened)
  • Device information for ad serving

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide URL shortening services
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Improve app performance and user experience

3. Data Storage

We do not store your shortened URLs permanently. They are processed in real-time and not saved on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob for advertisements
  • TinyURL API for URL shortening

Our app is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age.
